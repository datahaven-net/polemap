Frequently Asked Questions


  1. How do I get the GPS coordinates from Google Maps?
    Go to www.google.com/maps, zoom in on the place you want, then right click, then click on the first thing which is the GPS coordinates.

    Yes. Just put the pole code after https://polemap.ai/ in your Browser URL input. For example, if the code is U0 I9 just put https://polemap.ai/u0i9
